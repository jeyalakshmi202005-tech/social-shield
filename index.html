      Common Scam Types in India

      Know the threat before it finds you

      💰

      Lottery / Prize Scam

      Fake winning alerts asking for bank details or a "processing fee" to release your prize.

      💘

      Romance Scam

      Fake profiles build emotional bonds over weeks, then request emergency money transfers.

      💼

      Job Offer Scam

      Lucrative work-from-home offers requiring an upfront registration or training fee.

      🏦

      KYC / Banking Phishing

      Fake SBI / UPI alerts urging you to update KYC via a fraudulent link immediately.

      🎁

      Celebrity Giveaway

      Impersonated celebrity accounts promise doubled money or gift cards to steal credentials.

      📱

      OTP / SIM Swap

      Callers pose as bank staff and trick you into sharing OTPs to drain your account.
